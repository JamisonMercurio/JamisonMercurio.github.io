Platform:
I have put together a list of goals I will tackle as EUSA President, created based on a wide
variety of student feedback. Here are some of the ideas I will implement:

• Free Food and Drink: Unlimited haggis buffets in Teviot during Lounge Café hours, and bi-weekly whiskey
tastings free of charge throughout the semester.

• City Experiences: In order to help students experience Edinburgh’s history and culture, I’d like to subsidize
trips to various parts of the city. To that end, I will organize a giant end-of-term party in Edinburgh Castle,
with an open bar.

• Promoting Transparency: With classes, societies, and social lives, it’s hard for students to follow what is going on
at the EUSA officer level. Accordingly, I’ll organize an annual meet and greet with the EUSA Student President (me).

• Free Willy.

These are just some of the many accomplishments I hope to achieve over the course of the year as your EUSA president,
and I intend to update and prioritize this list accordingly based off of my conversations with fellow students.

I’m looking forward to talking to many more of you in the coming weeks ahead!
